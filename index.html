What Proof does

Simple by design.
Powerful over time.

No complicated plans. No diet logging. Just two gentle habits that help you finally see the change that's already happening.

One-tap gym logging
Every time you show up, tap the button. That's it. No details required — just a quiet record of the work you're putting in.
A streak that works for you
Your streak grows every time you visit. Miss a day? No guilt — you can earn it back. Proof is built to keep you going, not make you feel bad.
Monthly progress photos
Every 30 days, Proof gently reminds you to take a quick photo. Front view, side view — that's all. Stored privately, just for you.
Your transformation timeline
Scroll back through every month and see exactly how far you've come. The progress you couldn't see day to day — now impossible to miss.
A quiet space, just for you
No social feed. No followers. No comparison. Your photos and progress are completely private — seen only by you, always.
Gentle reminders
A kind nudge when it's time to log a visit or take your monthly photo. Encouraging, never pushy. Like a friend checking in on you.
How it works

So simple it actually sticks.

1
Open the app and log your visit
Every time you go to the gym, tap once to log it. No details, no tracking, no pressure. Just a record of the fact that you showed up — because showing up is everything.
2
Watch your streak grow
See your consistency build up over days and weeks. Your streak is a gentle reminder of how far you've already come — and a quiet encouragement to keep going.
3
Take your monthly photo check-in
Once a month, Proof reminds you to take a quick progress photo. Over time these photos become a private timeline of your transformation — progress worth looking back on.
